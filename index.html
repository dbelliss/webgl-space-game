Welcome to Spacefreight Crates Company!

Spacefreight Crates Co. is the galaxy's number 1 cargo retrieval company. Lost your goods in a dangerous asteroid field? No problem! SCC's highly trained drivers will have no problem recovering your cargo. And you just became one of those drivers!

For your first day on the job as a driver, you travel to the Asteroid Belt 55C to recover some cargo crates.
Some things to remember from your training:

  • 1. Collecting more crates lets you collect a bigger paycheck!
  • 2. Hitting anything other than crates will damage the ship, and repairs come straight out of your paycheck!
  • 3. Stunning any illegal crate collectors will allow you to collect their bounty, for even more pay!
  • 4. The space-proof crates can only withstand the vacuum of space for a short time, so you only have a few minutes to gather as much as you can!

Controls

  • WASD to change your movement direction
  • Spacebar to move forward (Release to slow down)
  • F to fire your laser
